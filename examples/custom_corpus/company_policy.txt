Acme Corp Security Policy v1.0

Password Requirements:
All employee passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase,
lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Passwords must be changed every
90 days. Never share your password with anyone, including IT support.

Access Control:
Access to sensitive systems requires multi-factor authentication.
VPN is mandatory for all remote access. Badge access logs are reviewed weekly.

Incident Response:
Report all security incidents to security@acme.example within 1 hour of discovery.
Do not attempt to remediate incidents independently.
