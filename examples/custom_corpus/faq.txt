Acme Corp IT FAQ

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit the self-service portal at https://id.acme.example/reset or contact
   the IT helpdesk at helpdesk@acme.example. You will need to verify your
   identity with a second factor before resetting.

Q: What should I do if I receive a suspicious email?
A: Do not click any links or open attachments. Forward the email as an attachment
   to phishing@acme.example and then delete it from your inbox.

Q: How do I request access to a new system?
A: Submit an access request through the IT portal. Your manager must approve
   all access requests. Access is granted based on least-privilege principles.

Q: What is the policy for using personal devices?
A: Personal devices (BYOD) are not permitted to access company systems except
   via the approved MDM profile and with explicit IT approval.

Q: How do I report a security incident?
A: Contact the security team immediately at security@acme.example or call
   the 24/7 security hotline. Do not attempt to contain or investigate
   the incident yourself.
