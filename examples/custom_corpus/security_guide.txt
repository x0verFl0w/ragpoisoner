Employee Security Awareness Guide

Phishing Awareness:
Never click links in unexpected emails. Verify sender identity before
opening attachments. Report suspicious emails to it-security@acme.example.

Data Handling:
Customer data must never leave approved systems. Encryption is required
for all data at rest and in transit. Personal devices may not store company data.

Physical Security:
Always lock your screen when leaving your desk. Tailgating is prohibited.
Visitor badges must be visible at all times.

Remote Work:
Use only company-approved VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.
Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN. Report lost or stolen devices immediately.
